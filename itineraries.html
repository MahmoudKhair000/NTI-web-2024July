El Ikhwa Islands commonly known as Brothers Islands, are two small Red Sea islands in the Red Sea Governorate of Egypt. The islands are a dive site featuring corals and two wrecks: Numidia and Aida. The islands are also famed for encounters with Oceanic Whitetip and Hammerhead Sharks.
