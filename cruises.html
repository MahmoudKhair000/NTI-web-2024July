Cruises

Heaven seems closer when you look at the sea.

Choose your adventure cruise and book the yacht for your group. Full charter booking with the minimum of 12 guests. If you are travelling solo, see the schedule of Individual places available on cruises and join a group.

Dive In Style

Imagine taking in all pleasures of a luxury cruise in the warm turqoise sea during the day and plunging in a different marina with its sparkling night-life each and every evening? Enjoy your luxury yacht and the tender Red sea with its array of pleasures: snorkeling, swimming, having fun on banana boat, sunbathing on a sky-deck of the yacht, pampering yourself on the beaches of lonely islands, fancy fresh food and exotic juices until sunset., then discover a new amazing marina every evening, new fairy tale of music and parties every night - Hurghada, El Gouna, Sharm-El-Sheikh - see and party them all.

Safari Dive In Style

is a perfect combination of adventure, diving and holidays. Spend a week onboard a luxury yacht, explore the best remote dive sites of the Red sea, relax in jacuzzi, enjoy perfect cuisine and service, get adrenaline of hot Egyptian party nights, dream away watching the stars and have an unforgettable diving week to the intact wrecks, colorful fishes, shoals of pelagics, sharks, mantas and pristine reefs of the Red sea.

Cruise In Style

Take in all what the Red sea has to offer, immerse yourself in the beauty of these breathtaking waters. There is everything for your getaway here – an elegant yacht, warm Jacuzzi under the stars to melt in, parties onboard and on the faraway islands around the bonfire, new gourmet experiences in the first-class restaurant onboard to revel, sheltered lagoons and white-sand beaches of deserted islands, lush coral gardens sparkling with millions of colors under the crystal-clear waters, schools of dolphins, 365-days-a-year sun, tender breezes, unforgettable sunsets and starlight nights in the middle of the sea.

Kitesurf In Style

Kite safari in Egypt is a grand journey to some of the world's most stunning waters, to remote uninhabited islands with vast turquoise lagoons - famous kitesurfing spots. It is an active sea vacation which suits everyone making you feel fit, fun and young. Conditions here are beyond expectation: steady reliable wind, mirror-flat, shallow, transparent, warm water, Egyptian sun and a unique atmosphere. The spots are suitable for all levels. For beginners it's a chance to learn a new exciting hobby safely. For experienced kiters it's a perfect opportunity to ride the waves without limits.
